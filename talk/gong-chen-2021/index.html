Long-time dynamics of small solutions to 1d cubic nonlinear Schrödinger equations with a trapping potential

Gong Chen
Zoom Link

Abstract

We will consider the long-time dynamics of small solutions to the 1d cubic nonlinear Schrödinger equation (NLS) with a trapping potential. I will illustrate that that every small solution will decompose into a small solitary wave and a radiation term which exhibits the modified scattering. In particular, this result implies the asymptotic stability of small solitary waves. The analysis also establishes the long-time behavior of solutions to a perturbation of the integrable cubic NLS with the appearance of solitons.
